manual of me

Conditions I like to work in?

  • I like to study in a quiet and calm environment
  • I like my work/studying to be structured
  • If I am working or coding which doesn't involve too much thinking, I like to have music on in the background

Times/hours I like to work?

  • 5 hours a day
  • I prefer to work between 10am-5pm
  • I am most productive and focused to learn between 10am-3pm
  • In the evenings I like to process and apply new information from that day

The best way to communicate with me?

  • In general, I feel that verbal communication is best. I think it is the quickest way to solve/troubleshoot a problem and avoid any misunderstanding
  • I'm also happy to chat via IM apps (slack, whatsapp etc)
  • If it is important information that doesn't require an instant reply then please email

The way I like to receive feedback?

  • I always welcome straightforward, honest feedback
  • I believe that any feedback, whether it is positive or negative, will help me to improve
  • I like to receive examples of how I can improve

Things I need?

  • A good night's sleep
  • Examples. I process new information better when given examples of how, why and where someting is being used
  • Time to walk or exercise to unwind and clear my head

Things I struggle with?
